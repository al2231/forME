Lo Loestrin FE

Lo Loestrin Fe is a low-dose combination birth control pill (oral contraceptive) containing two different female hormones.

Lo Loestrin Fe contains an estrogen called ethinyl estradiol and a progestin called norethindrone acetate.

Works by primarily suppressing ovulation, and thins the lining of the uterus, reducing chances of a fertilized egg implanting and growing

Pills are less effective than implants, injections, intrauterine devices and sterilization

Warnings

Does not protect against HIV infections (AIDS) or other STDs.

Take one pill at the same time every day until the pack is empty.
Do not skip pills even if you are spotting or bleeding between monthly periods or feel sick to your stomach (nausea).

If you missed One blue pill
Take it as soon as you remember. Take the next pill at your regular time. This means you may take two pills in 1 day.
